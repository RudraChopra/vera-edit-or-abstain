## Main changes since v5

- Added an admitted-shift stress test anchored to the stored real-data tables.
  The direct target-table rule violates 16 of its 36 accepted contracts under
  exact members of the learned bridge classes; strict MOSAIC releases the 20
  safe cases and abstains on all 16 failures.
- Added a measured retention-versus-robustness frontier over five registered
  error contracts, together with the distance of the harmful laws beyond the
  direct confidence regions.
- Added a 75-job scaling study over fine-token alphabets up to 64 states and up
  to four sources. The exact solver remains subsecond in every cell, and a
  constraint-generation stress uses 4 of 256 attacker assignments.
- Added a real ACSIncome eight-token extension and a numerical diagnosis of the
  primary-contract abstentions. The two relaxed-contract identity releases are
  explicitly labeled as degenerate no-op interfaces.
- Added numerical released-interface utility tables, a single checksum map,
  sharper bridge-centered novelty framing, and updated figures and abstract.
- Rebuilt anonymous and named main papers and supplements from common sources.
